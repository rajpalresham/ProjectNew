FELUCCA

Aswan is the usual boarding point for felucca rides, although you can also hop on at Luxor. DAHAB (RED SEA)

Dahab, on the Eygptian Sinai peninsular in the Red Sea, is a friendly place with some very good diving. It is most famous for its Canyon and Blue Hole dive sites.The diving in Dahab is mostly shore-based: the sea quickly drops off to great depths. More remote dive sites are reached by boat, or by camel.The Red Sea is very salty due to lack of rivers adding fresh water to the sea, and the hot weather causing evaporation to Dahab the (RED SEA).

STANLEY BRIDGE

It is the first bridge in Egypt to be constructed into the sea , with total length of 400 m. It constitute amajor part of the reconstruction and decoration of Alexandria cornich.this bridge thus it preserves the architectural style of the famous and cosmopolitan Egyptian city. It was inspired by the Montazah Palace with its Islamic style four towers at both ends of the bridge. Many people walk on the bridge to enjoy the view of the sea and the bay.
